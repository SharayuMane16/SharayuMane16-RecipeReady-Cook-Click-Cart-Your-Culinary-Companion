Thalipeeth Recipe

Recipe

Step1:Mix the ingredients into a pliable dough. Divide into 4 equal sized balls.

Step2:Heat a cast iron tava.

Step3:Place a piece of parchment on your work surface.

Step4:Place a ball of dough on it. Pat it down gently into a 5mm disc of about 10-15cm in diameter.

Step5:With your finger make a hole in the centre.Lift the thalipeeth togehter with the parchment, and flip it onto the hot skillet.

Step5:Peel off the paper and set aside. It can be re-used.

Step6:Drizzle a little oil around the edges of the disc on the skillet, and in the centre.

Step7:Cover with a lid and allow to cook until golden. Flip and cook the other side as well.

Step8:Serve with fresh cream or butter, a smear of hot thecha, and dahi.

Ingredients

1 cup thalipeeth bhajani (recipe above)

1 medium onion (1/2 cup), chopped

2 or 3 green chillies, chopped fine

Freshly chopped coriander

1/2 tsp salt

1/3 cup water to knead

Peanut oil, for frying
