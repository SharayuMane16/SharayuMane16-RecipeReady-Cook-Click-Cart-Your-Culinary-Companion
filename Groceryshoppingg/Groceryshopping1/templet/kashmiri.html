Mutton Rogan Josh

Ingredients

500 grams Mutton (medium-sized pieces)

Yogurt

Salt as per taste

Mustard Oil (you can use ghee instead of mustard oil)

Turmeric Powder

Red Chilli Powder

Fennel powder

Ginger powder

Asafoetida

Cumin seeds,

Cinnamon, Cloves

Peppercorns, & Cardamom

Recipe

Step1: Take medium-sliced meat in a pan and wash it properly with water.

Step2: Heat oil in a pressure cooker and add cinnamon, bay leaves, green cardamom, cloves, salt, asafoetida, and meat together.

Step3:Let the meat turns brown. After a few minutes, add a cup of water

Step4: Add red chilli powder along with turmeric powder to the meat. Keep stirring for about a minute.

Step5: Mix the curd nicely and add it into the pressure cooker.

Step5: Add water, fennel powder, ginger powder, and pressure cook for 2 minutes.

Step6: Peel & grind green and brown cardamom and add to the meat dish.

Step7: Finally, sprinkle cumin powder and simmer for a minute.

Kashmiri Saag

Ingredients

7 Tbsp Mustard oil

3 Brown cardamom

10 Kashmiri whole chillies

25 Whole garlic

250 gram Whole spinach

salt

Recipe

Step1: In a pressure cooker add mustard oil, brown cardamom, kashmiri whole chillies, whole garlic, whole spinach (saag), little salt and water

Step2: Put all of the above in a pressure cooker and let the spinach (saag) cook till 1 whistle.

Step3: Serve the Kashmiri Saag steaming hot.

Kashmiri khatte baingan

Ingredients

3 Tbsp mustard oil

1 cinnamon stick

1 Tbsp cumin seeds (zeera)

1 Tbsp kashmiri red chillies

1/2 tsp asafoetida (hing)

1/2 tsp turmeric powder (haldi)

1/2 tsp coriander powder (sookha dhaniya powder)

Salt, to taste

1 Tbsp dry ginger powder (saunth powder)

2 sliced onion

5 long brinjals

1 sliced tomato

1 tsp fennel seeds (saunf)

Coriander leaves, to garnish

1/2 lime

Recipe

Step1: In a wok (kadai) add mustard oil, cinnamon, cumin seeds, green cardamoms, kashmiri red chilies, asafoetida powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder and little salt. Mix them well together and add little water.

Step2: Add dry ginger powder and combine them together.

Step3: Add sliced onion and brinjals. Fry them well together.

Step4: Cover them and let the brinjals cook for 20 mintues.

Step5: Add the sliced tomatoes, fennel seeds and little water.

Step5: Let the tomatoes cook.

Step6: Sprinkle some coriander leaves and lime.

Step7: Serve the Kashmiri khatte baingan hot.

Dum Aloo

Ingredients

for ▢aloo preparation:

▢ 8 baby potatoes / aloo

▢ 2 cups water (or as required)

▢ ½ tsp salt

▢ 4 tbsp oil

▢ 1 tsp kashmiri chilli powder

▢ ½ tsp turmeric

For masala paste / gravy:

▢ 1 inch cinnamon stick (dalchini)

▢ 3 cloves

▢ 2 cardamom (elachi)

▢ ½ onion (thinly sliced)

▢ ¾ tsp ginger-garlic paste

v 1 large tomato (finely chopped)

▢ 10 cashews

For dum aloo recipe:

▢ 1 tsp oil

▢ ¾ tsp jeera / cumin seeds

▢ 1 - 1½ tsp chilli powder (adjust to your spice level)

▢ ¼ tsp turmeric powder

▢ ¾ tsp coriander powder

▢ salt to taste

▢ ¼ tsp cumin powder / jeera powder (optional)

▢ ¼ cup curd / yogurt

▢ 1 tsp kasuri methi / dry fenugreek leaves

▢ ¾ - 1 cup water (adjust to required consistency)

▢ few coriander leaves (finely chopped)

Recipe

Step1: In a kadai, add a tsp of oil along with cumin seeds

Step2: Once they splutter, add prepared masala paste and fry for 5-6 minutes.

Step3: Now add spices like chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder and salt to taste.

Step4: Continue to fry till oil separates.

Step5: Turn the flame to low and add curd. give a good mix.

Step5: Add 1 cup of water or as required to adjust the consistency.

Step6: Finally, add fried aloos and cook for 20 min.

Step7: Lastly add some crushed kasuri methi.

Step8: Garnish with coriander leaves and serve dum aloo hot with rice, chapathi or naan.

Kashmiri chicken pulao.

Ingredients

▢ 500 Gram Rice

▢ 6-7 Chicken thighs

▢ 2 tsp Cumin seeds

▢ 2 tsp Cumin seeds

▢ 2 tsp Coriander powder

▢ 3-4 pods Green cardamom

▢ 3-4 sticks Cinnamon

▢ 2 tsp Mace

▢ Few Black pepper corns

▢ 5-6 cloves Garlic, chopped

▢ 5-6 Raisins

▢ 1 cup Yogurt

▢ 2 tbsp Desi ghee

▢ 1 cup Onion, sliced

▢ 2 tsp Coriander seed powder

▢ 1 tsp Red chilli powder

▢ 1 Ginger, finely chopped

Cooking oil

▢ Salt & water

▢ 5-6 Green chillies, chopped

Recipe

Step1: Take rice, wash it properly and soak it in some water for 20 minutes.

Step2: Take 6-7 chicken thighs and cut it into even size pieces

Step3: Now wash these pieces well and drain the excessive water out, nicely.

Step4: Take 1 cup of yogurt and add salt, 1 tsp red chilli powder, 1 tsp turmeric powder, 3 tsp coriander seed powder and 1 tsp black pepper powder

Step5: Whisk the yogurt nicely and keep it aside.

Step6: Heat 2 tbsp desi ghee in a pan, add 4-5 cloves of garlic, add 5-6 cloves, a cup of onions and saute.

Step7: Now add the ginger and 5-6 green chillies and saute.

Step8: Now add the whisked yogurt mixture and saute well.

Step9: Cook for a few minutes and once you see the specs of fat coming up, add the chicken pieces.

Step10: Add some salt too at this stage.

Step11: Cook for a minute and add 2 cups of water and leave it to boil. Use enough of water because the rice will be cooked in this.

Step12: Let it simmer for a couple of minutes. When the chicken is cooked, add the rice to the chicken and allow it to cook.

Step13: Gently stir the rice because basmati after soaking becomes very fragile..

Step14: Once the rice is cooked, serve hot.

Kashmiri sheermal

Ingredients

▢ 2 + 1/2 cup All purpose flour

▢ 1/4 th cup Milk powder

▢ 1 teaspoon dry active Yeast

▢ 2 tablespoons Sugar

▢ 1/2 teaspoon Salt

▢ 1/2 cup Milk+ more for brushing or rubbing

▢ 1 Egg or 1/4th cup plain Yoghurt for a vegetarian option

▢ 3 tablespoons soft Ghee

▢ 1 teaspoon Kewra water

▢ 2 teaspoons or as required white Sesame seeds

Recipe

Step1: Mix together yeast and sugar with 1/4th cup of warm water. Keep aside for 7-8 minutes to bloom the yeast.

Step2: Mix together flour, milk powder and salt in a mixing bowl.

Step3: When yeast mixture blooms add cardamom powder and kewra water..

Step4: Also, add 1 egg or 1/4th cup of yoghurt to the yeast mixture.

Step5: Make a hole in the centre of the flour mixture and add in the prepared yeast mixture.

Step6: With your fingertips mix dry ingredient well with the wet ingredients.

Step7: Add-in milk(normal temp) in batches and knead well to make a semi-hard dough.

Step8: Spread the dough flat to a working surface.

Step9: Spread soft ghee over the dough as shown in the picture.

Step10: Now incorporate the ghee to the dough by dabbing and punching it with your hand.

Step11: Fold the dough and again knead to well incorporate the ghee.

Step12: Continue to knead to make a little soft and pliable dough.

Step13: Cover the dough and keep aside for about 2 hours or until the dough becomes double in size.

Step14: When the dough becomes double in volume, take out in a work surface and knead for few more minutes.

Step15: Preheat oven at 200 degree C for 10 minutes.Take one ball and roll into a thick chapatiPrick the chapati or disk as shown in the picture.

Step16: Rub 2 teaspoon of milk on the surface of the chapati, sprinkle some white sesame seeds and place it on the baking tray.

Step17: Bake them in preheated oven at 200 degree C for about 15 minutes or until the breads become dark golden brown in colour.

Step18: Sprinkle some milk and rub again the sheermal..

Step19: Serve with a cup of tea, and if you can serve with Kashmiri pink tea then it will be perfect. These sheermal are little sweet, soft and crumbly delicious bread. You can store them in an airtight container for 2-3 days..
